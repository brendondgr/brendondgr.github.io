Wire Fraud Warning

Cybercrime and wire fraud are serious threats in the real estate industry. We want to protect you and your funds.

Important Security Notice

Before wiring any funds, always carefully verify the wiring instructions. We will NEVER change our wiring instructions via email.

If you receive an email containing new or altered wiring instructions from anyone—even if it appears to come from us, your agent, or any trusted party—DO NOT SEND FUNDS.

What you should do:

  • Call us immediately using a verified phone number you obtained independently.
  • Do not use the phone number provided in the suspicious email.
  • Verbally confirm the account number and all instructions.
