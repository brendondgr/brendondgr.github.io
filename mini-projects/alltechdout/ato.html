Preventative Maintenance & Monitoring

Our managed services safeguard your office systems to ensure your operations run without interruption. We focus on prevention to save you from costly downtime and data loss.

  • Hardware Health Monitoring: We ensure your hardware is healthy and hard drives do not run out of space.
  • Automated Security Patching: All computers receive the latest security patches from Microsoft automatically.
  • Virus Scans: We use tamper-resistant antivirus software, included with our service.
  • Software Deployment: We ensure new software is deployed correctly and unauthorized programs are detected.
  • Fail-Safe Data Backups: State-of-the-art backup solutions to protect your critical data from any catastrophe.
