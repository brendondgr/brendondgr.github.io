# SEO Website Outline

## I. Foundation & Strategy Development

### Define Website Purpose and Business Requirements
- Establish clear business goals and user needs before building any SEO elements
- Conduct competitor analysis to identify content gaps and keyword opportunities
- Determine budget constraints and future marketing requirements to scope your SEO efforts appropriately

### Keyword Research and Intent Mapping
- Use tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, or Ahrefs to build a comprehensive keyword list
- Focus on long-tail keywords (3+ words) that reflect specific user intent and have lower competition
- Map keywords to search intent and create topic clusters with pillar pages connected to detailed subtopic content

## II. Technical SEO Infrastructure

### Site Architecture and Crawlability
- Create a logical hierarchy with clear categories that don't exceed reasonable page depth
- Generate and submit XML sitemap and optimize robots.txt file
- Implement structured data (schema markup) to help search engines understand your content

### Mobile and Speed Optimization
- Prioritize responsive website design for mobile-first indexing
- Optimize loading speed through browser caching, image compression, and code minification
- Compress and resize all media files before uploading to maintain small file sizes

### URL Structure
- Create descriptive, readable URLs that include relevant keywords and use dashes between words
- Keep URLs concise, logically constructed, and avoid long, irrelevant words or symbols

## III. On-Page SEO Elements

### Meta Tags and Headers
- Write unique title tags for each page that include main keywords and clearly describe page content
- Craft compelling meta descriptions that accurately represent page content
- Organize content with proper header tags (H1, H2, etc.) for hierarchy and readability

### Content Optimization
- Add your main keyword early in the content, especially in the first paragraph and H1 tag
- Use keywords naturally 3-4 times throughout content depending on length, avoiding keyword stuffing
- Create unique, high-quality content that fills competitor gaps and addresses user intent

### Image and Media Optimization
- Add descriptive alt text for all images and include keywords in filenames when relevant
- Use high-resolution images but optimize file sizes to balance quality and loading speed
- Avoid non-alpha characters in filenames and use dashes instead of underscores

### Internal Linking
- Link to relevant pages throughout your content using keywords for destination pages when possible
- Create a strategic internal linking structure that connects pillar pages to cluster content

## IV. Content Strategy and Creation

### Content Planning
- Make a list of topics based on your keyword research and build pages for each main topic
- Set up a blog and create a consistent publishing schedule
- Develop SEO content outlines for each piece that include recommended headers, optimal word count, and specific section requirements

### Content Quality Standards
- Publish amazing content that is extremely relevant to target keyphrases
- Maintain clean layout with short paragraphs and distinct sections to improve engagement
- Ensure all content is unique and not duplicated across press releases, blogs, or guest posts

## V. Off-Page SEO and Authority Building

### Backlink Strategy
- Build quality backlinks from reputable sources to establish trust with search algorithms
- Create a systematic link-building plan as part of your ongoing SEO efforts
- Use external linking strategically to relevant, authoritative resources

### Social and Brand Signals
- Integrate social media to amplify content reach and generate organic signals
- Monitor brand mentions and engage with your audience across platforms

## VI. Measurement and Continuous Improvement

### Tracking and Analytics
- Set up Google Search Console to track results and monitor site health
- Implement analytics to measure content success and user behavior

### Maintenance and Updates
- Stay current with SEO news and algorithm updates
- Regularly audit and update existing content to maintain relevance and rankings
- Continuously analyze competitor strategies and adapt your approach
